Cole Slaw

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shredded cabbage (about half a head)
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrot
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of apple cider vinnegar or 1 tablespoon of white wine vinnegar
  • Salt and pepper


How to cook

  1. Shred and combine the carrots and cabbage in a large bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the other ingredients.
  3. Mix dressing and veggies together and chill.
