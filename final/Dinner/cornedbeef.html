Corned Beef

Corned beef is great because it varies wildly in price over the course of the year, meaning you can get some great deals on it after St.Patricks day (when stores are overstockedo on it) and various other times. It can be used in a lot of different ways and is probably one of the easiest meals you can make. If you don't want a typical corned beef and cabbage dinner, try making a sandwich out of it using my recipe here!

Ingredients

  • 1 package corned beef
  • 1 half head of cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Potatos


How to cook

  1. Place the corned beef in a large pot and fill until it flows over the beef.
  2. If the beef came with a packet of spices, you can use that, or flavor it with whatever you would like!
  3. Put on low heat and simmer for 4-6 hours.
  4. Add cabbage, carrots and potatos to the pot and let cook for another 30 minutes.
  5. Drain the water and serve!
