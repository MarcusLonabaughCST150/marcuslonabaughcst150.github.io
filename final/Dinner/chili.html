Chili

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 diced bell peppers
  • 2 diced jalepeno peppers
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 cans of kidney or black beans
  • 1/4 cup chilli powder
  • 1/4 cup cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 6oz can of tomato paste
  • 1 14.5oz can of diced tomatos
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 teaspoons rice vinegar


How to cook

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, cook the beef until brown and drain.
  2. Stir in the onions, peppers, and garlic until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in the spices, tomatos and the beans.
  4. Stir in the 2 cups of water and the vinegar.
  5. Simmer for 1 hour before serving.
