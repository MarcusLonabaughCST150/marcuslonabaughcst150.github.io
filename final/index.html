The Lazy Cookbook

What is this website?

This is a website dedicated to helping people spend less money on food, learn how to cook, and save people time cooking! If you are getting bored of cooking the same recipes every night or want to avoid the shock when your shopping cart costs more than you expected, you can find plenty of ways to improve your culinary life on this site. Not only is this a resource for saving money, it is also a place to find great tasting food that won't cost you your entire night to make. Whether you are a lifelong cook or a college kid cooking for yourself for the first time, there is something on here to help you!

TheLazyCookbook.com is also dedicated not only to helping you make dinner. You will also find plenty of hearty, well rounded breakfasts to start off your day. Or, if you are in need if a mid-afternoon energy burst, you will also find great tasting lunches designed to get you through your day.

About Us

I am a developer who is passionate about two things: food, and saving money! I have spent many hours in my life comparing the costs of groceries, researching ways to save money on food, ways to make cooking go faster, and ways to make food taste better! After spending a long time bouncing from website to website, I decided to create a website to contain all of the things I have learned about cooking and shopping in one place.
