"Fridge" Omelette

This omelette recipe shows you the basics of creating an omelette, but remember that you can use absolutely anything you have lying around in it! Meats, veggies, and spcies of all kinds go great with eggs, and its up to you to experiment. Have fun with it! This recipe is a great way to use up leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheddar
  • 1/2 cup of chopped broccoli and carrots
  • 2 pieces of bacon
  • Salt and pepper
  • Whatever else you have lying around that you want to throw in!


How to cook

  1. Beat eggs and add salt, pepper and any other seasoning you want to add.
  2. Chop up the bacon and cook on medium-low heat.
  3. Add veggies and butter until vibrant or translucent.
  4. Add beaten eggs and let rest until the bottom is no longer runny. Cover for 2 minutes.
  5. Add cheese to half and fold over. Add more cheese on top and let it cook for another minute or two.
  6. Serve while still hot!
