Breakfast Burrito

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup of shredded cheddar
  • 1 tortilla
  • 2 tablespoons of salsa
  • Sprinkle of shredded lettuce
  • Salt and pepper


How to cook

  1. Beat eggs and add salt, pepper and any other seasoning you want to add.
  2. Heat the pan to medium and pour the eggs in.
  3. Move the eggs around with a spatula every time they start to get solid, breaking them up until they are no longer runny.
  4. Heat a separate pan to medium low and heat the tortilla for 1 minute on each side, sprinkling the cheese on the second side after flipping.
  5. Spread the salsa on the tortilla and add the lettuce.
  6. Add the eggs to the tortilla.
  7. Serve!
